About Our Program

Building Stronger Families Together

DUHIGURE MU MIRYANGO is Rwanda's innovative family-level Performance Contract system that brings the traditional concept of Imihigo into every household.

Our program empowers families in Kibungo Sector to:

  • Set and track family development goals
  • Assign individual responsibilities
  • Monitor collective achievements
  • Celebrate family successes together
